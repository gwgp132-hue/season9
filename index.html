Our Story

Cozy Dining with Family & Friends

Season 9 is a comfortable and clean place in Naranpura, Ahmedabad where you can enjoy hot Punjabi meals. We specialize in rich lentil dishes, traditional clay-oven tandoori breads, and tasty cheese starters.

We care about our customers' preferences, which is why we offer adjustable spice levels and a dedicated menu for **Jain food**. Along with Punjabi dishes, we also serve Chinese starters and warm soups.

Made in traditional clay tandoors
